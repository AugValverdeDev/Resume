About me

I am a skilled web developer with a strong background in graphic design. My experience includes several independent projects, working remotely as a Tester at PQ Latam and as a Graphic Designer at Vintage Publicidad and Parabola Studios.

With a solid foundation in graphic design, I have honed my skills using tools such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and After Effects, creating visually compelling designs and animations. My passion for technology and innovation led me to transition into web development, where I leverage my design expertise to build user-friendly and aesthetically pleasing websites. I focus on developing real-world projects that solve practical problems, and I aim to contribute to the open-source community and the share of knowledge in general.

I have an inquisitive mind and I'm always willing to explore new tech and to learn new tools and paradigms. My education includes graphic design from Atenea Institute and systems engineering coursework from Universidad del Valle. I am committed to continuous learning, as evidenced by my C2 English certification and ongoing self-study.
